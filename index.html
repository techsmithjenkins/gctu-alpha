Explore ForestGuard AI

Ghana’s rich forests — from the Atewa Range to Kakum and Ankasa reserves — are under constant threat from illegal logging and mining. Every year, our nation loses hundreds of thousands of hectares, fueling climate change and destroying biodiversity.

Traditional monitoring is costly and slow. ForestGuard AI delivers real-time detection and alerts, empowering communities and rangers to protect these ecosystems before it’s too late.

ForestGuard AI

Real-Time Forest Protection

ForestGuard AI is a real-time monitoring and alert system that detects illegal logging through sound classification and IoT sensors. It empowers governments, communities, and conservationists with instant alerts to stop deforestation before it’s too late.

  • 🔊 Sound Recognition: Detects chainsaws, vehicles, and unusual activity.
  • 🌐 IoT Connectivity: Enables remote forest monitoring.
  • 📊 Real-Time Alerts: Sends SMS, dashboard, and app notifications.
  • 🌳 Environmental Impact: Protects biodiversity and reduces deforestation.
